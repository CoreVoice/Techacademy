HexP Design System

Foundations and components for the HexP marketing site. v0.2 replaces solid greys with an opacity-based neutral ramp, expands purple/orange into full 100–900 scales, adds two proposed secondary hues, and corrects the gradient family to radial (matching Figma) with a grain overlay.

Neutrals — opacity-based, no solid greys

Every "off-black" is rgba(0,0,0,X) on the white/light surface. This replaces the old fixed --ink / --muted / --faint hex values entirely — one scale, nine steps, used for text, borders, dot patterns, everything that used to be a grey hex.

n-800rgba(0,0,0,.80)
n-700rgba(0,0,0,.68)

n-300rgba(0,0,0,.20)
n-200rgba(0,0,0,.12)
n-100rgba(0,0,0,.06)
n-900 = primary text (was --ink). n-700 = body copy (was --muted). n-400 ≈ old --faint tone. n-100 = hairline borders and the hero dot pattern.
